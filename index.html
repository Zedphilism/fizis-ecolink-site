Problem and value

  • Residents struggle to dispose bulky waste and schedule pickups
  • Info is scattered which leads to confusion and illegal dumping
  • Ecolink provides a hub for disposal info and micro jobs like yard cleanup and branch cutting

Scope and stakeholders

  • Phase 1 info hub and request posting
  • Phase 1 simple job board for residents and helpers
  • Stakeholders residents local council private haulers community helpers

Success metrics

  • Task completion rate per week
  • Average time to first bid
  • Reported illegal dumping near zero in pilot area
  • SUS at least 80 in pilot

Submission checklist

  • Clear problem statement
  • Personas two or more with tasks and pain points
  • Scope and out of scope
  • User stories and key flows
  • Metrics and validation plan
  • Team roles and responsibilities
