EcoLink — Usability Testing

Phase: Evaluation

This page documents the briefing script and task cards used to run usability testing on the EcoLink prototype for three core flows: booking a RORO bin, locating disposal centres, and lodging complaints.

Briefing Test Instructions Task Cards Recordings & Notes

Usability Testing Briefing

Thank you for participating in this usability test. Today, you will be interacting with a prototype of a system designed to help users:

  • Book a RORO bin
  • Locate waste disposal or recycling centres
  • Lodge waste-related complaints

Before we begin, please note that this is a prototype, not the final product. Some functions are not fully optimised yet. For example:

  • Attaching photos may not work as expected or may use placeholder screens.
  • Map accuracy and live location detection are limited and may not reflect real-world data.
  • Some buttons or pages may be non-functional or only partially interactive.
  • The system may not save data because it is not connected to a full backend.

These limitations are normal at this stage of development. We are mainly evaluating the interface design, clarity, task flow, and ease of use, not full functionality.

Test Instructions

During the test, you will be given three tasks to complete using the EcoLink prototype.

  1. I will read each task to you.
  2. You may read the task card in your own time.
  3. Please speak your thoughts aloud while using the system.
  4. If you feel stuck or unable to proceed, say “terminate”, and we will continue to the next task.

Remember, we are testing the system, not you. There are no right or wrong answers — your confusion or hesitation helps us improve the design.

Your screen and voice may be recorded for analysis, but your identity will remain confidential. Do you have any questions before we start?

Task Cards

These are the three main tasks used in the usability session. Each participant receives a printed version or on-screen card while performing the task.

Task Card 1 — Book a RORO Bin

Goal

You want to book a RORO bin for waste disposal at your location. Use the system to check the available bin options, select one, and complete the booking.

Task Card 2 — Locate Waste Disposal Centre

Goal

You want to find the nearest waste disposal or recycling centre. Use the system to search and identify a suitable disposal location.

Task Card 3 — Lodge a Complaint

Goal

You want to report a waste-related issue in your area. Use the system to locate the complaint section, choose the appropriate category, and submit a complaint with details.
